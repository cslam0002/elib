About Our Library

Our Library is more than just books on shelves. We are a vibrant hub for learning, discovery, connection, and growth, proudly serving our local community and its residents. Founded on the belief that free access to information and ideas is fundamental to a thriving society, we strive to empower every individual who walks through our doors or connects with us online.

Our Story: Roots and Growth

Our journey began decades ago, born from a shared community vision for accessible knowledge. From humble beginnings, we've evolved significantly, reflecting the changing needs of our patrons. Key milestones in our history include the construction of our current building, the expansion of digital services, and the development of special collections. Today, we operate multiple service points and serve thousands of patrons annually, continuously adapting to remain an indispensable community resource.

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission

To inspire curiosity, foster lifelong learning, provide equitable access to information, and strengthen the social and cultural fabric of our diverse community.

Vision

What We Offer: Your Gateway to Resources

Physical Collections

  • Extensive fiction and non-fiction for all ages (Adult, Teen, Children)
  • Magazines, newspapers, and periodicals
  • DVDs, Blu-rays, and audiobooks
  • Music CDs
  • Special Collections: Local history archives, genealogy resources, and unique materials

Digital Library

  • Thousands of e-books and downloadable audiobooks accessible 24/7
  • Streaming movies, TV shows, and music
  • Online magazines and newspapers
  • Research databases (academic journals, auto repair, language learning, test prep)
  • Digital literacy tools and online learning platforms

Technology & Equipment

  • Public computers with internet access
  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi
  • Printing, copying, and scanning services
  • Emerging technologies like 3D printers may be available

Beyond Books: Essential Services & Programs

Our dedicated team provides support and engagement through:

Expert Assistance

  • Reference help and research support
  • Personalized book recommendations
  • Technology troubleshooting

Robust Programming

  • Children: Storytimes, Summer Reading, STEM activities, early literacy programs
  • Teens: Clubs, workshops, volunteer opportunities

  • Exam proctoring

Making a Difference: Our Community Impact

We are committed to positive community impact through:

  • Education Partnership: Collaborating with local schools and early childhood centers
  • Economic Catalyst: Providing resources for job seekers, entrepreneurs, and businesses
  • Digital Equity Advocacy: Offering free internet, computers, and tech training
  • Cultural Hub: Hosting local artists, musicians, and cultural celebrations
  • Social Connection: Creating a safe, inclusive "third place" for residents

Our annual impact includes hundreds of free programs attended by thousands of residents, significant usage of public computers and Wi-Fi, high engagement in children's programs, and circulation of hundreds of thousands of physical and digital items.

Who We Are & How We Work

Our library is guided by an appointed board ensuring responsible governance. Our passionate team includes professional librarians, dedicated support staff, and volunteers. We are sustained by community support through municipal funding, grants, and the invaluable work of our Friends of the Library group. Tax-deductible donations are always welcome.
